# AI-Based Research Paper Generator and Reviewer

## Overview

The AI-Based Research Paper Generator and Reviewer is an intelligent platform designed to assist researchers, students, academicians, and professionals in creating high-quality research papers efficiently. The system leverages Artificial Intelligence (AI), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Machine Learning (ML) to automate the research writing process, provide intelligent suggestions, analyze paper quality, and improve academic productivity.

This platform helps users generate complete research papers from a topic or research idea, perform literature analysis, identify research gaps, generate citations, detect plagiarism, and review papers using AI-powered evaluation techniques.

---

# Problem Statement

Writing a research paper requires significant time and effort in literature review, content creation, citation management, formatting, and quality assessment. Many students and researchers struggle with identifying research gaps, organizing content, maintaining originality, and following publication standards.

This project aims to develop an AI-powered system that simplifies the research process by automatically generating and reviewing research papers while ensuring quality, originality, and academic compliance.

---

# Objectives

* Generate complete research papers from user-provided topics.
* Automate literature review generation.

* Generate citations and references automatically.
